Caloola Farm


Caloola Farm is located 50km south of Canberra at the base of Namadgi National park.
Our Farmstay is an alcohol and drug free environment at all times. All medications are to be provided to the office on intake and you medications will be dispensed as prescribed.
It’s a long way back to town with trips to the shops available every 3 days or during programs. We strongly encourage that you are prepared for being in a peaceful “away from the city life” program. Wifi is available.
